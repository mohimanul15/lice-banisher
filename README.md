Lice Banishers is a premium, service-oriented React landing page designed specifically for the New York City market. ## ðŸŽ¨ Design System

### Typography
- **Headings:** Serif (e.g., *Playfair Display*) - Communicates authority, trust, and luxury.
- **Body:** Sans-Serif (e.g., *Inter*) - Ensures high readability and a modern feel.

### Color Palette
- **Deep Navy (`#1A2E35`):** Used for the "Anchor" CTA and Footer to provide professional weight.
- **Teal (`#0D9488`):** Represents healing, calmness, and the botanical nature of the products.
- **Soft Pink/Sage:** Background accents to keep the site feeling warm and approachable.
